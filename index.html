About Me

I am an aspiring web developer and technology enthusiast with a strong interest in networking, system administration, and web technologies. My passion for technology started when I began learning how computers communicate within networks and how websites are built. I have developed practical skills in HTML5, CSS3, and responsive web design, which allow me to build websites that work well across different devices and screen sizes. I also have experience working with computer hardware, installing operating systems, and maintaining network equipment such as switches and routers. These experiences helped me develop strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills. I enjoy learning new technologies and improving my development abilities by building real-world projects. My goal is to become a skilled IT professional capable of developing modern web applications and managing efficient network systems.
